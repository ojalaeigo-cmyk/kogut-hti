Star King

Choose language

Star King is a simple mobile game: collect yellow stars and avoid red rocks.

Controls work the same with finger dragging and phone tilt: the base moves smoothly left and right.

You have 3 lives. Each red rock takes one life. After the third hit, the game is over.

⭐ Kollane täht — annab punkti.
🟥 Punane kivi — võtab ühe elu.
🎁 Boonuskast — iga 100 punkti järel juhuslik abi.

Every 100 points, a glowing bonus box appears. When collected, the game randomly chooses one effect: ⚡ red rocks vanish for 4 seconds, ❤ lives are restored, or 🌈 the base changes color. Difficulty: level 2-6 are 5% harder than the previous level; from level 7 onward each level is 10% harder.
